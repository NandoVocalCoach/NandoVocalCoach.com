About Me

Hi, I'm Nando, a Choir Conductor graduate from Tolima Conservatory with over a decade of experience as a Singing Teacher and Vocal Coach. I've dedicated my career to helping singers of all levels unlock their full potential.

My approach combines classical and modern techniques, tailored to your unique needs. I specialize in overcoming vocal challenges like cracking, strain, and limited range, and I'm passionate about helping you develop a healthy, sustainable, and joyful singing experience. My unique Vocal Workout Method is the result of years of experience and overcoming my own vocal challenges.

I understand that every voice is different. Whether you're an experienced performer or just starting your vocal journey, I'm here to guide you. I offer personalized training to improve your pitch, build stamina, expand your range, and develop your unique style. Let's work together to achieve your vocal goals!

Service
